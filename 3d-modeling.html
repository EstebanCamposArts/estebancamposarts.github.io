3D MODELING

Explore forms and create visual narratives that transcend the two-dimensional. Combine technical precision with artistic sensitivity, paying attention to every detail to achieve results that optimize the work of the person receiving the geometries and allow them to be manipulated according to their needs. I work from the initial reference research once I have the concept art to the final rendering, adapting to the project's objectives and the specific needs of each client. Blocking, sculpting, retopology, projection, fine detailing, baking and texturing with Substance, shading, lighting, and rendering with Maya.
