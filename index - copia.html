ABOUT ME

I am Esteban Campos, a graphic designer with over 20 years of experience in graphic design, branding, digital advertising, and commercial photography. My background has allowed me to develop projects for sectors as diverse as fashion, product design, and social media content creation, always focusing on quality and innovation. For six months, I gained experience in 3D modeling after training at Animum School, specializing in character creation for video games and film, using tools such as ZBrush, Maya, and Substance Painter. During my training, I participated in and was a finalist in The Rookies 2023 Draft, a distinction that reaffirms my ability to deliver high-quality work. I am committed to continuous improvement and high-quality projects.
